Topic: Health and Disease (Cancer)

Cancer is a disease in which some of the body’s cells grow uncontrollably and spread to other parts of the body. Cancer can start almost anywhere in the human body, which is made up of trillions of cells. Normally, human cells grow and multiply (through a process called cell division) to form new cells as the body needs them.

Cancer pain

1. Cancer pain has its own category
as it is unique in cause and therapies.

2. Patients with cancer pain may also face acute or
chronic non-cancer-related pain .

3. Generally patients with cancer pain do not exhibit
a single pain they may have multiple type of pain

4. One unique challenge in treating cancer pain is that
it is often a moving target with disease progression and
improvements in disease progression or worsening pain directly
stemming from chemotherapy ,radiation or immunotherapy .

Staging of cancer

Staging is important not only because it correlates with the patient’s long-term survival but also because it is used to determine which patient should receive adjuvant or neoadjuvant therapy . The TNM system is commonly used for classification to stage cancer. TNM stage may be used to indicate prognosis and guide treatment.
